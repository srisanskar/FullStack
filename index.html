Introduction

Hello! I'm Sanskar Srivastava, a student at IIT Bombay in Civil Department interested in CSE Department. I was kind of least active in my First Semester but can't say the same for Second Semester. I have explored many things throughout the First Year and it was kinda very fun, those all-nighters, gossips, laughs with the wingies were the best parts of my first year.

My Timeline

First Week

First week went in adjusting in everything from food to people. Also, the campus was a sight to watch during monsoons. And that's how life went enjoying until Mid Semester Exams.

Salsa Night

Heaven for couples. It rained on Salsa Night and we danced in rain, it was pure fun.

Marine Drive

We went to Marine Drive first time after coming in IIT Bombay and guess, to see what? Sunrise. BTW Sun sets at that side.

Navratri

Festive time begins with Navratri. Tried to do garba and dandiya but at last couldn't.

Halloween

I was the lead actor of Halloween GC trailer of 2024. Tried acting for the first time and it turned out to be really good. Also My first event as an Organizer.

Diwali

Wanted to go home during Diwali. But guess what? Chemistry Lab Practical on next day of diwali. Yup Sometimes academic schedule sucksss!!

First Semester Done

Finally First Sem got over and we got a sigh of relief. It was a sem full of orientations after all.

Lonavala

Due to all the stress we got during End Sem, finally we got a trip planned but guess what? timing not good. Lonavala wasn't lonavaling but we managed to chill in a villa with wingies.

TechFest

My first major Fest in the Institute and it was very very fun being a part of organizing team.

Mood Indigo

Asia's biggest cultural fest, no doubt. The concerts at night was the best part. Games were also on point, in short everything was on point and no doubt it was actually too much fun.

Traditional Day

Trad day was no doubt a vibe which is irreplacable. Crazyyy night it was.

Showstopper

Annual fashion showcase of StyleUp. Took part in that and our theme was based on Marvel v/s DC. I was Deadpool and costumes were a big mess and ultimately I had to body and face paint to get the look.

Holi

It was just water and colour on whole hostel building a night before holi. It was too much fun to run and escape the guards and fight with water and tear clothes of each other. Probably, the most wild festival in IIT Bombay.
